# Section 7.2 – Truncated Sample

This folder replicates the truncated-sample analysis of SSFA adoption (2007 cohort).

## Contents
- `data/real_log_nci.csv` – state-level log non-corporate income dataset.
- `scripts/TWFE_DiD_EventStudy_2007.R` – estimates TWFE, simple DiD, and event-study style DiD.
- `output/` – placeholder for regression tables and plots generated by the script.

## Results in Paper
- Tables 8–10 (TWFE, DiD, and Event-study estimates).
- Figures 4 and 8 (event-study plots).
